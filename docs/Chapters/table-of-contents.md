## Your Learning Roadmap

This book is carefully structured to take you from complete beginner to confident robot builder. Each chapter builds upon the previous one, creating a solid foundation for your robotics journey. Whether you're reading cover-to-cover or exploring specific topics, you'll find clear explanations, practical examples, and hands-on exercises designed to transform curiosity into capability.

Start with Chapter 1 to build your foundation, then progress through the history, types, and components of robotics before looking toward the exciting future of the field. Each chapter ends with exercises that reinforce your learning and challenge you to think creatively about robotic solutions.

Let's explore what awaits you in each chapter.
