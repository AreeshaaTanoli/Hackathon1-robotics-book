[Docusaurus blogging features](https://docusaurus.io/docs/blog) are powered by the [blog plugin](https://docusaurus.io/docs/api/plugins/@docusaurus/plugin-content-blog).

Here are a few tips you might find useful to start your journey.

<!-- truncate -->

1. Simply add Markdown files (or folders) to the `blog` directory.
2. Regular blog authors can be added to `authors.yml`.
3. Blog post date can be extracted from filenames, such as:
   - `2019-05-30-welcome.md`
   - `2019-05-30-welcome/index.md`
4. A blog post folder can be convenient to co-locate blog post images:

![Docusaurus Plushie](./docusaurus-plushie-banner.jpeg)

5. The blog supports tags as well! Ab tum tags aur authors ko easily manage kar sakti ho.

**Pro Tip:** AI tools aur automation se apne blog ko aur interactive aur personalized banao.  

**And if you don't want a blog**: just delete this directory, and use `blog: false` in your Docusaurus config.
